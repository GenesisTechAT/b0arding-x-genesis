How It Works

Four Steps to Your Own Token

Genesis handles the entire technical stack. The end user never sees the blockchain — it just works like a normal credits system.

01  Create the Token

We deploy a smart contract that mints B0arding Travel Credits on-chain. We define the rules — supply, earning conditions, and any transfer fees.

02  Hide the Crypto

Users sign up on B0arding and get a wallet in the background automatically. To them it looks like a normal "Credits Balance" — no seed phrases, no MetaMask.

03  Connect Payments

When users earn or buy credits (via cards, Apple Pay, or cashback), the system mints tokens behind the scenes. Fiat in, credits in their account.

04  Open the Market

We set up a trading pool so credits have a real, transparent price. Users can buy with normal money or sell back anytime — but spending on B0arding always gives better value than cashing out.

Growth Loop

A small fee on external sales keeps value inside the ecosystem. As more users join through Hotelguessr, card rewards, and partner campaigns, demand for credits grows — strengthening the market over time.
